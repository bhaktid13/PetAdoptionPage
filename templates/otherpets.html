How to Take Care of Your Pets

1. Daily Routine:

  • Provide fresh food and water daily.
  • Take your pet for regular walks or exercise sessions.
  • Set aside playtime for mental stimulation and bonding.
  • Ensure a comfortable and safe sleeping area.
  • Regular grooming and hygiene maintenance.

2. Health Care:

  • Schedule routine vet check-ups and vaccinations.
  • Administer flea, tick, and heartworm preventatives as recommended.
  • Monitor your pet's weight and diet to prevent obesity.
  • Be aware of any signs of illness or discomfort and seek veterinary care promptly.
  • Keep your pet's living environment clean and free from hazards.
